Lessons learned from my parents garden

Mon 22 July 2019
By Nicholas Onorato
Category: Thoughts
Tags: reflection gardening

My parents home is an acre in rural colorado. I've lived there the majority of my life and for the past 10+ years they've kept a large vegetable garden in the back yard. At times in my life I hated this garden. Because I had to help pull weeds when they had gotten out of control. That was my parents style, huge intermittent chunks of effort were thrown at the garden to keep it in shape. But having done that I began to learn lessons as I got older that I think will help me when I create my own garden.
